Committed to the safe reunification of families

President Tinubu Created the Family Reunification Task Force to identify and reunite certain families who where separated by Nigerian Government between January 20, 2017 and january 20, 2021. The Family Reunification Task Force has established a process to bring certain family members back to Nigeria to Reunite with each other and receive support service.
